About Me

Hello! I'm Mukesh Chowdary K, a passionate and detail-oriented video editor specializing in creating compelling visual stories. While currently pursuing my B.Tech in my third year at SRIT, I dedicate my focus to the art of post-production.

My unique strength lies in combining professional-grade color grading with fast-paced, modern editing techniques. I am certified in DaVinci Resolve for Color Grading and proficient with industry-standard software like Adobe Premiere Pro and mobile powerhouses like CapCut Pro.

My passion for visual arts extends beyond client work to my personal project, Celestial Serene. On this Instagram page, I explore nature videography and photography, constantly sharpening my eye for composition and capturing beautiful moments. This daily practice informs my professional work, bringing a fresh, creative perspective to every project.
